Are you looking for the best first-person shooters that prove the genre is alive, louder, and more intense than ever? The classic "dog days" of gaming gave us raw, unforgettable chaos with titles like Doom, Quake, and Unreal Tournament, forging a generation of shooters with unpredictability and style. Modern shooters haven't lost that sparkle!

From cinematic campaigns to insane multiplayer mayhem, here are the 18 best shooter games you can play right now based on the video:

---

## 1. battlefield 6

![Battlefield 6](https://media.contentapi.ea.com/content/dam/battlefield/battlefield-2042/images/2021/05/bf-2042-featured-image-reveal.jpg.adapt.crop191x100.1200w.jpg)

Battlefield 6 is back to big, chaotic battles that actually feel like a proper Battlefield game. The maps are massive, and the weather and day-night cycles shake up tactics. Smashing cover or buildings can completely change a firefight, making it fast, strategic, and fun. Portal mode adds another layer of creativity, letting you remix maps and game modes for whatever wild scenario you want.

## 2. warhammer 40,000: darktide

![Darktide](https://www.playdarktide.com/assets/images/og-image.jpg)

This follow-up to Vermintide 2 is a co-op slaughter fest, but its gunplay is surprisingly good. The Cantrell MG12 infantry lasgun tears through elites, and autoguns send hordes flying in satisfying ragdoll piles. It also features progression trees for every class and wave-based Mortis Trials for more firepower and fun.

---

## 3. borderlands 4

![Borderlands 4](https://borderlands.2k.com/images/share-image.jpg)

Keeping the chaos dialed up to 11, Borderlands 4 delivers stronger shooting, looting, and weapon variety. The grappling hook adds a slick new layer of mobility, making fights feel more dynamic. Co-op is still a blast, delivering that sweet, messy loot-filled fun whether you roll solo or with friends.

---

## 4. wolfenstein ii: the new colossus

![Wolfenstein II](https://images.ctfassets.net/6072tqx9clb9/58wF2eX8eY8G8k2Y4m4Y4C/c7e7b6d1c8c8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8/wolf-ii-nc-featured.jpg)

The combat in this game is loud, explosive, and incredibly satisfying. The ability to dual-wield any two weapons adds a chaotic, fun layer to the strategy. It also offers a deep, gripping origin tale for B.J. Blazkowicz, balancing serious emotional moments with a touch of absurd humor.

---

## 5. call of duty: black ops 6

![Black Ops 6](https://www.callofduty.com/content/dam/atvi/callofduty/cod-touchui/blackops6/common/social-share.jpg)

Black Ops 6 might just be the series hitting all the right notes. It features a surprisingly creative campaign, multiplayer amped up with omni movement, and a zombies mode that feels like the glory days again. With 90s cultural nods and punchy gunplay, it has something for every FPS fan.

---

## 6. counter-strike 2

![Counter-Strike 2](https://cdn.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/apps/csgo/images/csgo_react/social/cs2.jpg)

Despite the new name, Counter-Strike 2 keeps Valve's tactical shooter crown firmly on its head. With refreshed visuals, the real draw is its competitive depth and esports gold standard.

---

## 7. metro exodus

![Metro Exodus](https://www.metrothegame.com/media/2126/metroexodus_share.jpg)

Metro Exodus delivers an immersive mix of survival and shooting as you venture off the train into the Russian wilderness. Though venturing solo is tense, you have a creative arsenal of upgradeable weapons from crossbows to revolvers. The constant sense of moving forward keeps the slower-paced action engaging.

---

## 8. system shock (remake)

![System Shock](https://nightdivestudios.com/assets/images/games/system-shock/ss-share-fb.jpg)

This modern remake brings a 90s legend back to life. It operates as a tense immersive sim filled with exploration, inventory management, and terrifying encounters.

---

## 9. valorant

![Valorant](https://images.contentstack.io/v3/assets/bltb6530b271fddd0b1/blt72753ec309f48512/628e937d5789640ff73456c7/VALORANT_ANNOUNCEMENT_LOGOS_Social_Share_1200x630.jpg)

Valorant serves as the fun, entry-level cousin to more hardcore tactical shooters. It is more forgiving, more colorful, and easier to pick up if you are not a hardened competitive veteran.

---

## 10. superhot

![Superhot](https://superhotgame.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/superhot-share.jpg)

This is a tiny masterpiece that proves FPS games can be clever, stylish, and insanely fun all at once. The game heavily revolves around precision and timing.

---

## 11. halo: the master chief collection

![Halo MCC](https://images.ctfassets.net/6072tqx9clb9/2K2K6pD6vU6O6E6G6y2G6Y/8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e8e/halo-mcc-featured.jpg)

This collection is the ultimate way to dive into the John-117 saga. Years of server fixes, graphical upgrades, and technical tweaks have turned it into a polished experience that supports up to 120 FPS.

---

## 12. doom eternal

![Doom Eternal](https://bethesda.net/en/game/doom-eternal/assets/images/share.jpg)

Doom Eternal captures the right mix of aggression from the Doom Slayer. It runs beautifully on nearly every platform, whether you're tearing through demons at 120 FPS on PC, playing on Xbox One, or shredding spines on a Switch.

---

## 13. titanfall 2

![Titanfall 2](https://media.contentapi.ea.com/content/dam/titanfall/titanfall-2/images/2016/06/tf2-multiplayer-featured-image.jpg.adapt.crop191x100.1200w.jpg)

Titanfall 2 is renowned for its incredible momentum and movement mechanics, letting you effortlessly chain wall runs. It also features an unforgettable single-player campaign anchored by the bond with the mech BT-7274.

---

## 14. s.t.a.l.k.e.r. 2: heart of chornobyl

![Stalker 2](https://www.stalker2.com/assets/img/og-image.jpg)

This game nails the series' quirky hardcore DNA. It makes exploration, scavenging, and survival genuinely tense and rewarding, even if the punishing mechanics and chaotic AI misbehavior are just part of the charm.

---

## 15. half-life 2

![Half-Life 2](https://cdn.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/220/header.jpg)

Half-Life 2 may be old enough to drive, but it hasn't lost its magic. It remains a masterclass in physics and pacing, proving that elements like the gravity gun are completely timeless.

---

## 16. elite dangerous

![Elite Dangerous](https://www.elitedangerous.com/assets/images/og-image.jpg)

On the surface, it's a space simulator where you can trade, fight, explore, or mine. But spend more than a few hours in its vast galaxy, and it becomes an experience centered on absolute freedom.

---

## 17. escape from tarkov

![Escape from Tarkov](https://www.escapefromtarkov.com/assets/images/og-image.jpg)

Escape from Tarkov delivers a brutal, high-stakes, hardcore first-person survival game. It mixes elements of tactical combat, looting, and base building into an unforgiving world where every choice matters.

---

## 18. hunt: showdown

![Hunt Showdown](https://www.huntshowdown.com/assets/images/og-image.jpg)

With sound design that has won awards for its realism and ability to heighten tension, this game is beautiful and terrifying. Mouse aiming provides the precision needed for the high stakes combat, where every shot counts whether you're stalking prey or escaping with the bounty.
